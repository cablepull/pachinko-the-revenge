How to play

If you've never seen a Japanese pachinko machine, here's everything you need.

The premise

You're sitting at a digital pachinko cabinet. Hold the chucker knob (SPACE); the machine fires balls in a continuous stream. Most balls miss. Occasionally one lands in the central chucker (the gold cup labelled HESO), which triggers a reel spin. Match figures across the reels and you hit a jackpot — the attacker doors open and a fixed payout follows.

That's the whole loop. The texture is in between: each spin can escalate into a reach — an animation that telegraphs "this might be the one" — and the longer/louder the reach, the higher the chance the reels actually line up.

What you'll see on screen

  • 3 reels (LCD) the result is the middle row. Three matching figures = jackpot. Three matching 7s = FEVER.
  • REACH banners a spin escalated. Calm = polite tease (~2% hit). Mid = real chance (~25%). Premium = serious (~70%). Confirmed = almost certain (~95%).
  • ATTACKER the door below the LCD. Opens during a jackpot to receive the payout. ~1,440 balls per 16-round jackpot.
  • KAKUHEN a 165-spin "chance time" window most jackpots enter. Jackpot rate jumps from 1/200 to 1/36. Cabinet tint turns red. Don't waste it.
  • DATA LAMP top-right HUD: spins since last jackpot (regulars hunt machines with high counts), kakuhen status, the last 10 jackpot intervals.
  • CHAPTER the revenge plot advances with each jackpot. New chapter = new reach animations unlocked.

The math (real spec sheet)

  • Base jackpot 1 / 199.8 per spin
  • Kakuhen jackpot 1 / 35.9 per spin
  • Kakuhen entry 70% of jackpots enter kakuhen
  • ST window 165 spins, deterministic
  • Payout 16 rounds × ~90 balls = ~1,440 per jackpot

What you should be doing

Hold SPACE. Watch for reaches. Get to kakuhen. Try to chain jackpots inside the 165-spin window before it expires. Unlock all four chapters.

There is no money, no leaderboard, no save-across-sessions yet. This is a single-cabinet study in pachinko grammar. Press H to bring this panel back any time.
