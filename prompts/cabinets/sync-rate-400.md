# Prompts — `sync-rate-400` cabinet (シンクロ率400)

**Archetype:** Premium (Eva-inspired biomech)
**Mechanic:** SyncRateMeter (fills during reach activity → guaranteed confirmed at threshold)
**Palette:** Deep space blue #0b1438, Biomech orange #f06030, Sterile white #e8eef8, Sync purple #8c5cdc

## Style anchor

A premium-IP biomechanical cabinet. Starfield deep-space aesthetic with
biomechanical "interface" elements (cables, panels, signal patterns).
Eva-inspired but with ORIGINAL character design — no EVA Unit silhouettes,
no NERV logo, no Asuka/Rei/Shinji-recognizable figures. The cabinet
should feel HIGH-STAKES, deliberate, cinematic. Deep space blue dominant;
biomech orange + sterile white for HUD-like accents; sync purple as the
catharsis accent.

The cabinet's emotional tone is "the operation has begun. Sync rate
rising. Pilot, prepare for contact." Cinematic deliberation, NOT
chaos.
