# Prompts — `thunder-herald` cabinet (雷鳴の使者)

**Archetype:** Brutal (Hokuto-inspired martial arts)
**Mechanic:** BattleSequence (confirmed reach becomes a skill-check)
**Palette:** Charcoal #1a1815, Dust orange #c66c2a, Cracked red #8a1818, Lightning yellow #fff066

## Style anchor

A post-apocalyptic martial-arts cabinet. Wasteland aesthetic — cracked
earth, dust storms, lightning. Hokuto-no-Ken-inspired but with original
character design (NO Kenshiro, NO Fist of the North Star likenesses).
The cabinet should feel HARSH, dangerous, kinetic — the opposite of
deep-sea-song's gentleness. Dust orange + cracked red dominant; lightning
yellow as the catharsis accent.

The cabinet's emotional tone is "the storm has been building for years
and it breaks tonight." Bombastic, intense, EARNED through long base play.

## thunder-herald#back-panel-v1 — 1024×768 back-panel

```
A stylized post-apocalyptic wasteland rendered as a 2D backdrop. The
horizon is a low cracked-earth line in the lower third — angular,
geometric "cracks" in dust orange revealing charcoal beneath. Above
the horizon, a turbulent sky in shades of charcoal and cracked red —
NOT smooth, but cell-shaded into 3-4 distinct band-shapes suggesting
a dust storm.

Lightning bolts in lightning-yellow fork down through the sky — 3-5
deliberate, angular lightning forms (NOT random photo lightning;
hard-edged vector lightning). Each bolt is single-pixel-line at its
thinnest, 4-pixel-line at the trunk.

In the mid-distance: a single jagged silhouette suggesting a ruined
structure (NOT a literal Tokyo Tower or any recognizable landmark)
in dark charcoal. Around it, drifting dust motes — small dust-orange
specks scattered diagonally.

Composition: 1024×768 landscape, full-bleed. The CENTER AXIS is open
(no detail in the central upper area where the LCD overlays; no detail
in the central lower area where the chucker sits). Detail is in the
upper-corners (sky / lightning), lower-edges (cracked earth), and
mid-left + mid-right (drifting dust, the ruined structure).

Atmosphere: harsh, kinetic, dangerous. NOT mysterious or quiet — this
sky is ALIVE with weather.

Style: hard-edged vector art, flat shading, 4-color palette (charcoal
#1a1815, dust orange #c66c2a, cracked red #8a1818, lightning yellow
#fff066). 1024×768 landscape.

NEGATIVE: characters, faces, people, vehicles, real-IP likenesses
(no Hokuto Kenshiro, no Mad Max), text, kanji, signage, watermarks,
photorealistic, painterly, soft edges, bloom, lens flare, JPEG
artifacts, gradient banding, more than 6 distinct colors,
center-of-frame detail, gentle/calm atmosphere.
```

## thunder-herald#cut-in-confirmed-v1 — 512×768 character cut-in

The cut-in fires during a confirmed reach AND during the
BattleSequence mini-game. The character is the protagonist — the
"thunder herald."

```
A stylized warrior figure mid-motion, occupying the LEFT THIRD of a
512×768 portrait canvas. The figure is in dynamic pose — one fist
raised, weight shifted forward, coat / scarf flaring behind. The
silhouette is charcoal-dark with dust-orange rim-lighting along the
upper-left edge (light from upper-left).

The figure is muscular, lean — NOT bulky. Wears post-apocalyptic
combat gear: layered coat, fingerless gloves, headband or wrap
around the brow. No detailed facial features — the face is in
shadow except for one fierce eye visible (cracked red).

Around the figure: dynamic LINE WORK in lightning yellow — a
"power-up" effect of 5-8 angular lightning streaks emanating from
the raised fist outward and upward. The streaks are hard-edged
vector lightning, not particle-blur.

Background (right two-thirds): mostly alpha-transparent with 2-3
dust-orange diagonal speed lines and a faint cracked-red atmospheric
wash. NO full scene background.

The figure must read as CHALLENGING the player — this is the moment
they raise their hand and the world holds its breath. NOT a portrait
photo; NOT a brand mascot.

Style: hard-edged vector, flat shading, 4-color palette (charcoal
#1a1815, dust orange #c66c2a, cracked red #8a1818, lightning yellow
#fff066) plus alpha. 512×768 portrait with alpha channel.

NEGATIVE: face details (the eye is a simple shape), photorealistic
muscle anatomy, multiple figures, full background, text, watermarks,
real-IP likenesses (Hokuto Kenshiro / Mad Max / Goku / any famous
martial-arts character), gentle expressions, modern street clothes.
```
