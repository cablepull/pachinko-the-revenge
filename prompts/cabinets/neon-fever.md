# Prompts — `neon-fever` cabinet (ネオン・フィーバー)

**Archetype:** ChromeRetro (Fever-inspired vaporwave)
**Mechanic:** FeverChain (every Nth jackpot becomes a chained auto-fire mini-mode)
**Palette:** Magenta #ff2da6, Cyan #00d4ff, Chrome silver #d8d8e0, Sunset yellow #ffcb50

## Style anchor

A 1980s vaporwave / synthwave / chrome-retro pachinko cabinet. The
original Fever (1980 SANKYO) homage rendered through a vaporwave lens.
Sunset gradients, chrome reflections, grid horizons, palm tree
silhouettes — the Miami Vice + Tron + Outrun mood. Magenta and cyan
dominant; chrome silver for catharsis-momentum; sunset yellow as
the high-energy accent.

The cabinet's emotional tone is "kick the door open. Lights on.
FEVER!" Loud, bright, no shame.

## neon-fever#back-panel-v1 — 1024×768 back-panel

```
A stylized 1980s vaporwave landscape rendered as a 2D backdrop.
Lower half: a perspective GRID HORIZON in cyan-on-magenta — the
classic Outrun synthwave grid receding into the horizon. The grid
lines are hard-edged single-pixel cyan over a magenta-to-sunset-yellow
3-step gradient background suggesting a sunset sky.

Upper half: a 3-step gradient sky (magenta at top, sunset yellow at
horizon) with one large stylized chrome-silver SUN disc — a perfect
circle with horizontal bands across it (the classic "chrome sun"
vaporwave element). The sun sits center-upper, slightly to the right.

Mid-distance silhouettes: 2-3 stylized PALM TREE silhouettes (one
left, one right, one mid-distance behind the sun) in magenta — hard-
edged vector palms, NOT detailed botanical illustrations.

Scattered through the scene: small chrome-silver geometric shapes —
floating triangles, diamonds, the occasional rectangle — that
suggest "abstract retro shapes" without being literal objects.
About 8-10 distributed across the scene.

Composition: 1024×768 landscape, full-bleed. The CENTER AXIS is
deliberately balanced — the chrome sun sits center-upper, the
horizon line bisects the canvas, the palms frame the edges.
This is the ONE cabinet where center-of-frame detail is OK because
the LCD overlay will *contrast against* the sun rather than hide it.

Atmosphere: LOUD, bright, unashamed retro. NOT subtle, NOT
brooding. The vaporwave manifest.

Style: hard-edged vector art, flat shading with 3-step gradients
on the sky and grid, 4-color palette (magenta #ff2da6, cyan #00d4ff,
chrome silver #d8d8e0, sunset yellow #ffcb50). 1024×768 landscape.

NEGATIVE: characters, faces, people, text, kanji, vaporwave-aesthetic
text (no "fever" or "neon" rendered in pixel font), real-IP
likenesses, watermarks, photorealistic palms, photorealistic
sunset, painterly, soft edges, lens flare, bloom (the chrome sun
is hard-edged, NO glow halo), JPEG artifacts, gradient banding
beyond the deliberate 3-step gradients, more than 6 distinct
colors.
```

## neon-fever#cut-in-confirmed-v1 — 512×768 character cut-in

The cut-in fires during a confirmed reach AND the FeverChain
trigger. The "character" here is abstract — a "Fever Spirit" —
because the vaporwave aesthetic doesn't have a single human
protagonist.

```
A stylized abstract figure occupying the LEFT THIRD of a 512×768
portrait canvas. The "figure" is a chrome-silver geometric form —
roughly humanoid in proportion but constructed from interlocking
hard-edged triangles, diamonds, and rectangles. Suggests a "fever
spirit" or "fever construct" — NOT a literal human.

The form is rendered as polished chrome silver with magenta
rim-lighting along the upper-left edge and cyan rim-lighting
along the lower-right (two light sources, the vaporwave staple).
"Eye" position is a single sunset-yellow circle, NO facial
features.

Around the figure: a halo of 6-8 hard-edged magenta-and-cyan
geometric shapes (triangles, diamonds) emanating outward in a
sun-burst pattern — the "FEVER" energy made visible.

Background (right two-thirds): mostly alpha-transparent with
2-3 hard-edged cyan grid-lines (echoing the back-panel's grid
horizon) and scattered chrome-silver dots. NO full scene
background.

The figure must read as ABSTRACT POWER — not a human, not a
character with a name, but a manifestation of the fever state
itself.

Style: hard-edged vector, flat shading, 4-color palette (magenta
#ff2da6, cyan #00d4ff, chrome silver #d8d8e0, sunset yellow
#ffcb50) plus alpha. 512×768 portrait with alpha channel.

NEGATIVE: human face details, photorealistic chrome reflections
(use hard-edged shading instead), multiple figures, full
background scenes, text, watermarks, real-IP likenesses
(no Daft Punk helmets, no Tron Light Cycle rider), painterly,
soft edges, organic forms (this is GEOMETRIC chrome, hard-edged).
```
